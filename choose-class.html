Warrior Class

Embody the role of a mighty frontline combatant, wielding massive swords and shields, ready to charge in and deliver devastating blows.

KEY ATTRIBUTES

Primary Attribute:

Strength (STR)

Primary Weapon:

Sword, Axe, Spear, Polearm

Hit Points (HP):

Very High

Mana Points (MP):

Low

Pros:
  • Warriors are the tanks of MapleStory. They have high HP and defense, which makes them great for taking on tough enemies.
  • They also deal massive damage, especially when it comes to melee attacks.
  • Warriors have a simple and straightforward playstyle, which makes them easy for beginners to pick up.
Cons:
  • Warriors can be slow and lumbering, which makes them vulnerable to fast-moving enemies.
  • They also lack range, which can make it difficult to take out enemies from a distance.
  • Warriors can be a bit boring to play, since their skillset is limited compared to other classes.

Archer Class

Become a skilled bow-wielder, mastering the art of shooting arrows with accuracy and speed, allowing you to take down enemies from a safe distance.

Primary Attribute:

Dexterity (DEX)

Bow, Crossbow

Hit Points (HP):

Medium

Mana Points (MP):

Medium

Pros:
  • Archers are the ranged attackers of MapleStory. They have high accuracy and critical rate, which makes them great for taking out enemies from a distance.
  • They have a variety of skills that allow them to shoot arrows in different directions and deal massive damage.
  • Archers have a unique playstyle that involves managing their positioning and using their mobility to dodge attacks.
Cons:
  • Archers have low HP and defense, which makes them vulnerable to attacks.
  • They can struggle in close combat, since their attacks are focused on dealing damage from afar.
  • Archers can be a bit tricky to play for beginners, since their skills require more precision and timing than other classes.

Unleash magical mayhem as a magician, wielding powerful spells to defeat hordes of monsters with spellbinding precision.

Intelligence (INT)

Wand, Staff

Low

Pros:
  • Mages are the magic users of MapleStory. They have a wide range of spells and abilities, which makes them versatile in combat.
  • They can deal high damage from a distance, which makes them great for taking out enemies before they can even get close.
  • Mages have a unique playstyle that involves managing mana and cooldowns, which can be challenging but rewarding.
Cons:
  • Mages have low HP and defense, which makes them vulnerable to attacks.
  • They can be tricky to play for beginners, since their spells require more strategy and planning than other classes.
  • Mages can be reliant on consumables and gear to maintain their mana pool and damage output.

Pros:

  • They have a wide variety of skills that allow them to sneak around undetected, stun enemies, and deal massive damage.
  • Thieves have a fast-paced and exciting playstyle that keeps players on their toes.
Cons:
  • Thieves have low HP and defense, which makes them vulnerable to attacks.
  • They can be tricky to play for beginners, since their skills require more precision and timing than other classes.
  • Thieves can struggle against enemies with high health, since their attacks are focused on dealing burst damage.
